About Me

My name is Craig Mars and I love the outdoors. I currently live in Cape Town, the most beautiful city in the world. I am a massive fan of hiking, making my way through the different trails on Table Mountain and other routes in the Boland.


I also enjoy horseback riding in the summer time, either along the riverside in the Echo Village or in the winelands in Stellenbosch. On rare occasions we do the sunset out-rides on the beach in Paternoster.


When I'm in the comfort of my home, I enjoy cooking and playing tabletop boardgames with those closest to me. An evening also wouldn't be complete without some console gaming!
